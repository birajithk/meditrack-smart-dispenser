# Problem Statement

Medication non-adherence is a common problem among elderly patients and long-term medicine users. Patients may forget to take medicine, take it at the wrong time, or select the wrong medicine compartment.

Existing low-cost solutions such as phone alarms and manual pill boxes mainly provide reminders or organization, but they do not verify whether the medicine was actually taken. Caregivers also lack a simple way to remotely monitor medicine adherence.

This project proposes an embedded smart medicine dispenser that reminds the user, provides access to the correct medicine compartment, verifies pill removal, logs intake events, and notifies a caregiver when a dose is missed.
